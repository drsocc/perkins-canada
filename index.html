Summary

The most direct path from Brian C. Lynch in the United States to Canada runs through the Bernier maternal line. Brian's 2x great-grandfather, Achille "Archie" Bernier, was born in Trois-Rivières, Quebec, Canada in 1877 and immigrated to the United States in 1890 at approximately age 13.

Before Archie, the Bernier family lived in Quebec for seven continuous generations (c. 1693–1890), centered around Charlesbourg and Bellechasse County. The line traces back to André Bernier, who immigrated from Niort, France to Quebec in 1693.

Other Canadian connections in the Perkins spreadsheet: Stephen Perkins (4x great-grandfather, born 1800 "East Canada"); Phoebe Anna Merchant (step-great-great-grandmother, born 1871 Canada); and the entire Merchant/Marchand line, which traces back to Jacques Marchand in Trois-Rivières in 1660. The Bernier line is the most direct because it is only two generations removed from Brian.
